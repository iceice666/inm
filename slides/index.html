01 — Foundations

Four anchors, tuned into UI tokens.

How the palette works

Quiet by default, clear on intent.

Hierarchy comes from tone and border, not from color noise or heavy shadow. One action color does the signalling; everything else stays calm.

1

Warm canvas

Stone in light, plum in dark — never pure black or white.

2

Clay is the signal

The single accent for actions, links, and active state.

3

Mist for state

A cool support tone for quiet statuses and metadata.

inm — rollout10

How adoption works

Four steps to a shipped palette.

1
Drop in the tokens
Add the OKLCH variables and wire the light and dark roots — no build step required.
2
Build on the scale
Compose buttons, inputs, rows, and badges from the shared semantic tokens.
3
Tune the contrast
Check APCA across every surface; nudge muted and accent until both modes read clean.
4
Hand it off
Export the Tailwind config and document the system in a portable DESIGN.md.
